Privacy & Cookie Policy

At Global EcoNews, we take your privacy seriously. This policy outlines how we handle data and your rights as a visitor to our network.

1. Data Collection & Transparency

We do not require users to register or provide personal information to browse our news. Personal data such as names and email addresses are only collected when voluntarily submitted through our "Contact & Partnership" forms. We use this information solely for the intended purpose of the communication.

2. Advertising & Third-Party Cookies

We use Google AdSense to serve advertisements on content-rich pages. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites. These cookies allow Google to provide relevant financial and educational advertisements to our audience.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

3. Technical Log Files

Like many other Web sites, Global EcoNews makes use of log files to ensure network security and site performance. This information includes IP addresses, browser types, and timestamp data. This information is not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.
